## App Description

### Description (4000 characters max)

```
TrustCare helps you find trusted healthcare providers and make informed decisions about your health. Read verified patient reviews, discover nearby doctors and clinics, and get AI-powered health guidance—all in one app.

KEY FEATURES:

🔍 Find Trusted Healthcare Providers
• Search across 99 medical specialties including family medicine, cardiology, dermatology, dentistry, and more
• Discover providers near you with interactive map view
• Filter by location, specialty, ratings, and verified status
• View detailed provider profiles with services, hours, and contact information

⭐ AI-Verified Patient Reviews
• Read authentic reviews from real patients
• Advanced AI verification detects and prevents fake reviews
• Comprehensive ratings covering wait times, bedside manner, treatment efficacy, and facility cleanliness
• Submit your own reviews to help others make informed choices
• Optional review verification with appointment documentation

🤖 Rehber - Your AI Health Guide
• Get symptom-based specialty recommendations
• Receive guidance on when to seek care vs. home treatment
• Emergency detection with immediate 112 connection
• Available 24/7 in your preferred language
• All conversations remain private and secure

✓ Provider Verification & Claims
• Healthcare providers can claim their profiles
• Verification through professional credentials (medical licenses, certifications)
• Verified badge ensures profile authenticity
• Providers can respond to reviews and update their information

🌍 Multi-Language Support
• Available in 5 languages: English, Turkish, German, Polish, and Dutch
• Seamless language switching within the app
• Localized content for better accessibility

🔒 Privacy-First Approach
• Your health data belongs to you
• Export all your data anytime in JSON format
• Secure storage with industry-standard encryption
• No data selling to third parties
• Transparent privacy practices

📊 Comprehensive Provider Profiles
• View ratings, reviews, and verification status
• See services offered, accepted insurance, and pricing
• Check opening hours and contact information
• Get directions with integrated maps
• Call or visit directly from the app

WHO IS TRUSTCARE FOR?

TrustCare is perfect for anyone looking for quality healthcare:
• Patients seeking a new doctor or specialist
• Families moving to a new city or country
• Travelers needing medical care while abroad
• Healthcare shoppers comparing providers
• People with chronic conditions managing ongoing care
• Anyone wanting to share their healthcare experiences

WHY TRUSTCARE?

Healthcare decisions are too important to leave to chance. TrustCare combines the power of community reviews with AI verification to ensure you're getting authentic, helpful information. Our platform covers everything from general practitioners and dentists to specialists and hospitals—all with the transparency you deserve.

Whether you're looking for a family doctor, searching for a specialist, or just browsing reviews before your appointment, TrustCare gives you the information you need to take control of your healthcare journey.

COMPLETELY FREE TO USE

TrustCare is free to download and use. No subscriptions, no hidden fees. We believe everyone deserves access to trustworthy healthcare information.
```
