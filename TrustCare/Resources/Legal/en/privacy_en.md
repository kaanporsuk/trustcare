# Privacy Policy

## Data we collect
- We collect account details you provide during sign-up.
- We store profile data such as name, language, and preferences.
- We may process provider interactions and saved items.
- Technical logs may be used to improve reliability and security.

## How we use data
- Data is used to deliver core TrustCare features.
- We use information to personalize language and content.
- Usage insights help us improve product quality.

## Data sharing
- We do not sell your personal data.
- Data may be shared with service providers needed to run the app.
- We may disclose data when required by law.

## Your rights
- You can request access to your personal data.
- You can request corrections to inaccurate profile information.
- You can request deletion of your account and related data.
- You can contact support for privacy-related requests.

## Cookies and similar technologies
- We may use local storage for settings and session continuity.
- These technologies help maintain secure authentication.
- You can clear app data by signing out and removing app data locally.

## Security
- We use safeguards to protect personal data.
- No system is perfect, but we continuously improve security practices.
- Report suspected security issues to support promptly.

## Contact
- Reach out to support for any privacy questions.
